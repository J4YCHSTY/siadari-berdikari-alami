About Siadari Berdikari Alami

Siadari Berdikari Alami is a company that specializes in unique Indonesian decorative items. We’ve been in existence since 2023 and have positioned ourselves as the provider of environmentally friendly products that blend traditional beauty with modern practicality. Jakarta, Indonesia where we are located, is just one hour away from the major port of Tanjung Priok.

Currently we have two categories of premium products, which are bamboo baskets and pandan bags. bamboo and pandan. both sets come in different types and sizes

we have established partnerships with several experienced local craftsmen in tasikmalaya west java who have a long tradition of making bamboo and pandan handicrafts. this makes it possible for us to provide best service at incredibly competitive prices to ensure product quality, we conduct strict quality control(qc) on each order item, even though for thousands of order items.

TERMS & CONDITIONS

Payment Methods

To build mutual trust, we recommend the following payment methods:

  • Irrevocable Letter of Credit (L/C) at Sight (for amounts above USD 20,000)
  • Telegraphic or Swift Wire Transfer (T/T)
    • A 40% down payment is necessary to begin production. The remaining amount must be paid upon invoice after the container is loaded. Upon receipt of the transfer, the consignee will receive the original documents.

    Order Terms

    FCL (Full Container Load) The minimum order is 1 x 20 feet container with a combination of products (maximum 3 items).

    Sample Order

    We provide free samples to our clients with amount under 100 USD. Just provide us with your courier account. However, to avoid unfair business practices, we will make a selection first before providing free samples.

    Price Terms

    Available price term isFOB (Free On Board) only. The shipping port is Tanjung Priok, which is 1 hour from our factory.
