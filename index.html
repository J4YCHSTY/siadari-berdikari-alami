About Us

Siadari Berdikari Alami is a company that specializes in unique Indonesian decorative items. We’ve been in existence since 2023 and have positioned ourselves as the provider of environmentally friendly products that blend traditional beauty with modern practicality. Jakarta, Indonesia where we are located, is just one hour away from the major port of Tanjung Priok.

Currently we have two categories of premium products, which are bamboo baskets and pandan bags. These both sets come in different types and sizes according to what is best for you or makes your work comfortable.

We have established partnerships with several experienced Tasikmalaya local craftsmen in West Java who has a long tradition of making bamboo and pandanus handicrafts. This makes it possible for us to provide best service at an incredibly competitive price to ensure product quality, we do QC strictly on each order item, even though for thousands of order items

Our Values

Quality

Quality is our foundation at Siadari Berdikari Alami. Each decorative item, from bamboo baskets to pandan bags, is crafted with meticulous attention to detail by skilled artisans from Tasikmalaya. Our rigorous quality control ensures that every product meets high standards, building trust and loyalty among our customers.

Quantity

We recognize the need for a diverse range of products. By offering various types and sizes of our items, we cater to different customer preferences while efficiently fulfilling bulk orders. Our collaboration with local craftsmen allows us to maintain a steady supply without compromising quality.

Competitive Pricing

We believe in making high-quality decorative items accessible. By optimizing our supply chain, we offer our unique products at competitive prices, ensuring affordability while upholding our craftsmanship standards. This strategy helps us reach a broader audience.

Timeliness

Timeliness is crucial for customer satisfaction. Our location, just an hour from Tanjung Priok port, enables efficient logistics and quicker delivery times. We prioritize meeting deadlines for all orders, reinforcing our reputation as a reliable partner.

Sustainability

Sustainability is central to our mission. We use renewable materials like bamboo and pandan, minimizing environmental impact. Our eco-friendly practices ensure that we contribute positively to the environment and support responsible consumption.

Collaboration

Collaboration drives our success. We partner with local artisans to preserve traditional techniques while fostering community support. By building strong relationships with customers and suppliers, we enhance our product offerings and services.

Payment Methods

To build mutual trust, we recommend the following payment methods:

  • Irrevocable Letter of Credit (L/C) at Sight (for amounts above USD 20,000)
  • Telegraphic or Swift Wire Transfer (T/T)
    • A 40% down payment is necessary to begin production. The remaining amount must be paid upon invoice after the container is loaded. Upon receipt of the transfer, the consignee will receive the original documents.

    Order Terms

    FCL (Full Container Load) The minimum order is 1 x 20 feet container with a combination of products (maximum 3 items).

    Sample Order

    We provide free samples to our clients. Simply provide us with your courier account. However, to avoid unfair business practices, we will first conduct a selection before providing free samples.
